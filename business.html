Frequently asked questions

Browse answers to your questions on program access, billing, and Mindvalley Membership. Need more help? Reach out to us at our Support Center and we’ll get back to you ASAP.

What is the difference between Mindvalley Business membership and Mindvalley membership?

Mindvalley provides an innovative learning platform for personal transformation, and our business membership expands upon this by offering an organizational solution tailored to facilitate growth for individuals, teams, and companies. Mindvalley cultivates a growth-oriented mindset among individuals and teams by emphasizing holistic well-being and fostering balanced development across all aspects of life. Through various Business plans, organization members gain access to quests, programs, workshops, live sessions, and calls, enabling teams to track progress both at the individual and organizational levels.

Can my team members complete the quests at their own pace?

Yes! That’s the beauty of Workway. Your employees will get unlimited access to 100+ hours of video course material that they can work through at their own pace. Because everyone experiences life differently, we encourage your employees to handpick the episodes that better serve them.

Do my team members need to be together for team-based Quests?

No they don't. While quests are delivered in daily lessons that each member of your team takes in the same day, each person can choose the time that best suits them.

What support is available for my team?

If you opt-in for organization or enterprise plans, your team will be assigned a dedicated Account Manager. The main goal of your Account Manager is to provide you and your team members with a smooth learning experience, they will be available to support you and your team no matter what comes up.

How can I decide which plan is right for my team?

We are here to help you! If you would like to speak to someone directly or if you are looking for a discounted enterprise deal and a custom set-up for your employees, you can send an email to betterteams@mindvalley.com.

Will I have access to reporting as an organization admin?

Most definitely! As the organization admin, you will have the overview of engagement and completion data that keeps you updated on the progress of your team. The data is only available on group level, to keep the confidentiality on individual level.

What does the Workshop mean?

In the context of a business like Workway, which specializes in personal growth and team empowerment, a "Workshop" typically refers to an interactive session designed to enhance skills, foster teamwork, and promote personal and professional growth among employees. Workshops are practical, engaging, and aimed at applying learning in real-world scenarios.

Who are the facilitators of those workshops?

The facilitators of such workshops are usually experts in their respective fields, including trainers experienced in corporate training and personal development. Facilitators might also include seasoned professionals who have a background in transformative education and have demonstrated profound impacts on personal mastery and team dynamics.
