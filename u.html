What makes Mindvalley U different?

We guarantee the experience of a lifetime

Connect

You’ll find your people and feel truly seen

This isn’t networking—it’s coming home. From welcome hugs to late-night soul talks, you’ll meet the kind of humans who make you feel instantly understood. Entrepreneurs, artists, parents, dreamers—people on fire for growth.

You won’t just make friends. You’ll build your tribe for life.

Transform

You’ll unlock the next version of yourself

Here, you don’t just learn—you evolve. Through powerful sessions led by world-class teachers, you’ll break old patterns, shatter limiting beliefs, and step into a new version of yourself.

One with more clarity. More courage. More purpose.

Impact

You’ll take the stage and inspire others

You didn’t come to play small.

At MVU, you don’t just sit in the crowd—you can stand up and speak.

Share your story. Teach a session. Move the room.

Many of today’s top Mindvalley authors started right here—on this very stage. Why not you?

Share

You’ll grow alongside the people you love

This is transformation for the whole family. While you’re evolving, your kids are too—with our immersive Kids & Teens Program designed to spark curiosity, courage, and emotional intelligence.

You’ll bond in ways no holiday ever could. These are the memories that define a lifetime.

Celebrate

You’ll learn hard, play harder

Breakthroughs by day. Magic by night. We’re talking legendary themed parties. Rooftop sunsets. Fireside conversations that turn into lifelong connections. This is where growth feels like joy. And every moment feels like a story you’ll tell forever.

Experience Mindvalley U at Tallinn’s iconic Kuulturikatel (Tallinn Creative Hub)

Join us in the heart of Tallinn at Kultuurikatel, a stunning creative hub set in a historic power plant where industrial heritage meets modern innovation. This iconic venue will be the central stage for transformative talks, hands-on workshops, and immersive programs designed for adults, teens, and kids.

You’ll fall in love with Amsterdam—and yourself

Amsterdam isn’t just a city. It’s a vibe. A portal. A place where creativity flows like the canals, and every cobblestone invites a new chapter.

From morning bike rides along tree-lined paths to impromptu café chats that turn into life-changing connections, Amsterdam has a way of pulling you into the present moment—and back into who you really are.

It’s bold. It’s beautiful. It’s buzzing with culture, color, and possibility. And for 14 extraordinary days, it becomes your playground for reinvention.

Explore world-class museums. Lose yourself in historic neighborhoods. Dance by the water at golden hour. In a city where every corner holds magic, you don’t just learn differently—you live differently.

This year, Amsterdam is more than a destination. It’s the perfect stage for your transformation.
